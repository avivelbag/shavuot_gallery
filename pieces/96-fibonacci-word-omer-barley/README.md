# Seven Times Seven, Plus One

**Piece 96 — Shavuot Gallery**

An animated Fibonacci word fractal that makes the 49-day Omer count visible in the self-similar geometry of the barley harvest. The Fibonacci word (generated by the substitution 1→10, 0→1 for 18 iterations, yielding F(20) = 6765 characters) is interpreted as turtle-graphics instructions, producing a space-filling fractal curve that tiles the plane. A gold tracer moves along the curve, leaving a 49-segment glowing trail and flashing Hebrew numerals at every completed Omer cycle.

## Connection to Shavuot

- **Barley and the Omer.** The Omer is a sheaf of barley (Leviticus 23:10–11) whose wave offering inaugurates the forty-nine-day count toward Shavuot. Barley seeds arrange themselves according to Fibonacci phyllotaxis — the same mathematics that generates the Fibonacci word fractal.

- **The 7×7 structure.** The forty-nine days are seven weeks of seven (Leviticus 23:15), a structure the Sefer HaChinuch (Mitzvah 306) describes as Israel's preparation — like a bride counting days before her wedding with the divine at Sinai.

- **Self-similarity as theology.** The Fibonacci word fractal is self-similar: any segment contains the geometry of the whole. This mirrors the Omer's own recursive structure — each week builds on the previous, the seventh week completing what began on day one.

## Technical approach

- **Fibonacci word:** 18 substitution iterations from "1", using 1→10, 0→1. Result: F(20) = 6765 characters stored as `Uint8Array`.
- **Turtle graphics:** For each character at index i, advance one step. If character is '0', turn left (−90°) if i is even, right (+90°) if i is odd. Turn angle: 90°.
- **Scaling:** Compute bounding box after full walk; scale fractal to fill 80% of canvas.
- **Performance:** Static fractal drawn once to an offscreen canvas using `globalCompositeOperation = 'lighter'` (overlapping segments bloom into bright gold). Each animation frame blits the offscreen canvas and redraws only the 49-segment trail and tracer dot.
- **Color:** Segments colored by `(i mod 49) / 49` mapped through a gradient: deep wheat gold (#8B6914) → barley green (#6B8C3E) → harvest amber (#D4A200).
- **Animation:** Tracer advances 1 segment/frame. Trail fades linearly over 49 segments. At every 49th segment, a Hebrew numeral (א through מ״ט) flashes with a 30-frame fade-in/fade-out.
