# Piece 22 — Before the Words

**"Akdamut Millin"** — The 22 letters of the Hebrew alphabet assemble one at a time from a cloud of 300 gold particles on a midnight-blue canvas, each letter converging over 1.5 seconds, holding, then scattering to reform as the next. After all 22 letters cycle, the opening words of the Akdamut piyyut — אַקְדָּמוּת מִלִּין ("Before the Words") — assemble and hold for 3 seconds before the sequence restarts.

**Technique:** canvas 2D particle-swarm letter assembly, Hebrew/Aramaic typography

**Theme:** Tikkun Leil Shavuot / Torah and Devotion

**Palette:** background #080d1a (midnight blue-black), particles #e8c060 (soft gold), glow #ffdd44
