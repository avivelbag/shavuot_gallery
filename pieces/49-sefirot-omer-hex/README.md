# Seven Times Seven — The Sefirot Omer Grid

**Theme:** Sefirat HaOmer  
**Technique:** hexagonal grid canvas animation  
**Source:** Leviticus 23:15–16; Sefer HaChinuch Mitzvah 306; Tikkunei Zohar

An animated hex grid of 49 hexagons (7 weeks × 7 days), each colored by the Sefirah of its week and labeled with its Hebrew day number. Hexagons bloom into view one by one. A 50th glowing hexagon at the center represents Shavuot — the fiftieth gate that stands outside the 7×7 matrix.

Hover any hexagon to see its Sefirah combination in Hebrew.
