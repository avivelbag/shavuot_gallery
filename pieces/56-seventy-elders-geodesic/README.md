# They Saw God and Ate — Geodesic Crown of the 70 Elders

**Piece 56 · Shavuot Gallery**

A slowly-rotating 3D geodesic wireframe icosphere rendered via pure JavaScript perspective projection (no WebGL). Eighty triangular faces — product of one frequency-2 subdivision of an icosahedron — are projected to canvas 2D each frame using a 3×3 rotation matrix and perspective divide.

Seventy amber glowing nodes mark the 70 elders of Exodus 24:9-11 who ascended Sinai and beheld God at the covenant meal. Ten brighter white nodes at the apex mark Moses, Aaron, Nadab, and Abihu. Edges are depth-shaded (closer = brighter). A ground-plane ellipse at the base of the dome evokes the mountain camp.

**Source:** Exodus 24:9–11 — *vayechezu et haElohim vayochlu vayishtu* ("and they beheld God and they ate and drank")

**Theme:** Matan Torah / Covenant at Sinai

**Technique:** 3D geodesic wireframe (pure JS projection) — icosphere subdivision, rotation matrix, perspective divide, painter's algorithm, radial gradient glow nodes
