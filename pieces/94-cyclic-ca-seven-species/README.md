# Seven Waves Across the Field

**Theme:** Bikkurim / seven species (shivat ha-minim)
**Technique:** Cyclic cellular automaton (7-state Moore neighborhood, threshold K=3)

A cyclic cellular automaton on a 200×160 grid where each of the seven states represents one of the seven species of the Land of Israel: wheat gold, barley tan, grape purple, fig brown, pomegranate red, olive green, date amber. From random initialization, spiral and target wave fronts self-organize within ~500 frames, each state converting neighbors to the next species in a perpetual harvest cycle.

The piece visualizes the Bikkurim offering brought to the Temple on Shavuot, as described in Deuteronomy 8:8 and the declaration of Deuteronomy 26. The wave propagation mirrors the way the harvest front moved northward and upward across the Land of Israel during the Omer weeks — barley first in the Jordan Valley, wheat last in the highlands at Shavuot.
