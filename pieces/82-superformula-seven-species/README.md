# In the Shape of Abundance — Superformula Seven Species

**Technique:** Gielis superformula / parametric shape morphing  
**Theme:** Bikkurim / seven species / Chag HaKatzir

The Gielis superformula r(θ) = (|cos(mθ/4)/a|ⁿ² + |sin(mθ/4)/b|ⁿ³)^(−1/n1) generates all
seven silhouettes — wheat, barley, grapes, figs, pomegranate, olive, dates — from a single
equation with varying parameters. The canvas cycles through the Shivat HaMinim with smooth
parameter interpolation and Hebrew labels, each shape morphing visibly into the next.

The seven species (Deuteronomy 8:8) are the material basis of the Bikkurim first-fruits
ceremony brought to the Temple on Shavuot. Their abundance is the sign of the covenantal
gift of the Land.
