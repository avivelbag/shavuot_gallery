# 51 — As One Person, With One Heart — Am Segulah at Sinai

Boids flocking simulation. ~400 agents follow separation, alignment, and cohesion rules to produce emergent collective murmuration around a fixed Sinai light at the canvas centre.

## Theme

Am Segulah / Covenant — Exodus 19:2 (Rashi: *ke'ish echad be'lev echad*) and 19:5–6 (*Am Segulah*, kingdom of priests). Individual autonomy producing emergent unity maps the Sinai paradox: collective covenant, individual reception.

## Technique

Canvas 2D boids flocking simulation — O(N²) neighbour search with N=400, Float32Array buffers, soft trail rendering (partial alpha clear), chevron boid rendering, radial Sinai glow gradient.
