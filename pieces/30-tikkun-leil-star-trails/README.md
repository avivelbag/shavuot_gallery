# The Night Turns Toward Morning — Tikkun Leil Star Trails

A WebGL fragment shader piece tracing the arc of Tikkun Leil Shavuot — the
all-night Torah vigil from nightfall to dawn.

A procedural star field rotates slowly around a fixed pole star, each star
leaving a short luminous arc as the night sky turns. The sky color transitions
over a sixty-second loop: from deep midnight blue through astronomical twilight
violet, pre-dawn rose, and amber sunrise, before fading back to night. A dark
hill silhouette with lit windows grounds the celestial rotation in a human
scene. The Hebrew phrase **עַד-עֲלוֹת הַשַּׁחַר** — *until the rise of the
dawn* — is drawn from the Zohar's description of the vigil.

**Theme:** Tikkun Leil Shavuot / Night Vigil and Dawn  
**Technique:** WebGL fragment shader / procedural star field, GLSL star trails, 2D canvas overlay
