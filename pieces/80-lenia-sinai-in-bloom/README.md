# And the Mountain Blossomed

**Shavuot theme: Greenery and flowers / Har Sinai in bloom**

Lenia continuous cellular automaton on a 128×128 toroidal grid with the Orbium parameterization (μ=0.15, σ=0.017). A smooth ring kernel drives each cell's growth function, producing glider-like blossoms that emerge from random seeds and drift across a green field — enacting the Midrashic tradition (Pirkei de-Rabbi Eliezer ch. 41; Shir HaShirim Rabbah 1:2) that Mount Sinai burst into flower at the moment the Torah was given.

## Technique

Canvas 2D — Lenia continuous cellular automaton with smooth ring kernel convolution, ImageData pixel rendering, nearest-neighbor upscaling.
