# Seven Weeks of Seven — Spirograph Omer Rose

**Piece 31** | 2026

**Technique:** canvas 2D — hypotrochoid spirograph (roulette curve)

**Theme:** Sefirat HaOmer — the seven weeks of seven days

A seven-petaled spirograph rose traced by hypotrochoid equations with parameters R=7, r=1, d=3. The curve is drawn progressively over eight seconds, shading from field green (barley, Passover) to harvest gold (wheat, Shavuot). When complete, the Hebrew word שָׁבוּעוֹת appears at the center where the seven petals converge, surrounded by the forty-nine day-numbers of the Omer.

The seven-fold symmetry encodes the kabbalistic structure of Sefirat HaOmer: seven weeks of seven sefirot, each day a unique combination of two divine qualities on the path from liberation to revelation.
