# Keter Torah — Crown of Torah Harmonograph

**Theme:** Keter Torah / Crown of Torah — Shavuot as the receiving of the Torah crown

**Technique:** Harmonograph — dual-pendulum Lissajous decay curves, progressive canvas animation

## Description

A simulated dual-pendulum harmonograph traces dense, crown-like Lissajous curves as both pendulums decay. Four overlapping traces in royal blue, gold, violet, and white accumulate at low opacity, building jewel-like brightness at symmetry axes. The curve writes itself progressively at ~60 fps over 6–8 seconds, holds, then fades and restarts with slightly different damping for variety.

Frequency ratios (x: 3, y: 2 and x: 2, y: 3) produce a pointed-arch, crown-like form with bilateral symmetry. Phase offsets of π/6 between traces build depth.

Hebrew text כֶּתֶר תּוֹרָה appears centered below the canvas in gold.
