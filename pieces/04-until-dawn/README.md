# Until Dawn — Tikkun Leil Shavuot

Hebrew letters from the Torah rain down through a canvas that transforms from the
deep blue of midnight to the rose-gold of dawn, tracing the arc of Tikkun Leil
Shavuot — the tradition of studying Torah through the night until morning.

Each letter falls at its own pace, rotating gently like a spark from the heavens.
Letters shift from near-white candlelight at the top of their descent to warm amber
as they approach the ground and dawn nears.

At the midpoint of the 90-second cycle the phrase **נַעֲשֶׂה וְנִשְׁמַע**
("We will do and we will hear" — Israel's answer at Sinai, Exodus 24:7) rises into
view and fades, marking the hinge between night and dawn.

**Theme:** Tikkun Leil Shavuot, Matan Torah  
**Technique:** Generative Hebrew typography, Canvas 2D
