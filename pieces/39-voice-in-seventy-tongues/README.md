# Piece 39 — The Voice in Seventy Tongues

**"Kol Echad L'khol Echad"** — The divine voice at Sinai split into seventy languages simultaneously (Shemot Rabbah 5:9). This piece renders a Penrose P3 rhombic tiling — an aperiodic tessellation of fat and thin rhombuses that radiates from a central aleph (א). The tiling is generated algorithmically by deflating 10 golden triangles in a "sun" configuration 6 times, producing 640 triangles that fill the canvas with infinite non-repeating variety from a single origin point.

**Technique:** Penrose P3 aperiodic tiling via Robinson-triangle deflation, canvas 2D animation

**Theme:** Matan Torah / The Voice in Seventy Languages — Shemot Rabbah 5:9

**Palette:** background #1a0c02 (desert night), thick tiles parchment #FFF8E8 to gold #C89030, thin tiles sky blue #B8D4E8 to deep blue #1A3A6A; central aleph warm white

**Animation:** slow sinusoidal pulse of luminosity radiating outward from center, ~8-second cycle; no tiles move, only brightness shifts
