# 71 — The Abundance of First Fruits

**Theme:** Bikkurim / First Fruits  
**Technique:** iterative circle packing / canvas 2D

Iterative random circle-packing: 600 candidate centers are seeded at random;
each grows outward until it would overlap a placed circle or the canvas boundary,
then is accepted at its maximum radius. The result is a dense mosaic of harvest
colors keyed to the seven species (shivat haminim) of Deuteronomy 8:7–10.

The essay traces Shavuot's three Torah names — Chag HaShavuot, Chag HaKatzir,
Yom HaBikkurim — through the bikkurim ceremony and the rabbinic derivation of
Birkat HaMazon from Deuteronomy 8:10: eat, be satisfied, then bless.
