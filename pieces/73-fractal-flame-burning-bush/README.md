# The Bush That Was Not Consumed — Fractal Flame Burning Bush

A fractal flame rendered by the FLAM3 chaos game algorithm: five weighted affine transforms with swirl and sinusoidal variation functions, accumulating 8 million iterations into a 700×700 float32 histogram. Log-density tonemapping converts hit counts to brightness; a fire palette (crimson → deep orange → hot gold → near-white) is blended by the per-pixel average transform color. The result is a luminous, flame-like structure rising from the bottom of the canvas, with curling tendrils and a glowing core — evoking the Burning Bush of Exodus 3.

**Theme:** Har Sinai  
**Technique:** fractal flames / IFS accumulation histogram  
**Scripture:** Exodus 3:1–4, 3:14; Deuteronomy 4:10  
**Midrash:** Exodus Rabbah 2:5; Rashi on Exodus 3:2
