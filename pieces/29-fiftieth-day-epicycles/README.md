# Piece 29 — The Fiftieth Day

**"The Fiftieth Day — Fourier Epicycle Hebrew Calligraphy"** — The Hebrew word שָׁבוּעוֹת (Shavuot) is rendered by Fourier epicycles on a deep sapphire canvas. The outline of the letterforms is sampled as a sequence of 2D points; a Discrete Fourier Transform decomposes the sequence into frequency components; and the animation draws concentric rotating circles — arm by arm from largest to smallest — whose combined tip traces the word in gold and ivory.

**Technique:** Fourier epicycles / DFT-driven animation, canvas 2D

**Theme:** Shavuot — the Fiftieth Day / Counting the Omer

**Palette:** background #0B1A3D (deep sapphire), spinning circles #C8962A (gold) at 40% opacity, traced path #F0ECD8 (warm white)
