# 43 — The Second Tablets: A Celtic Knot of Renewal

Grid-based Celtic knotwork in the shape of the two tablets (luchot), rendered as an animated ribbon with 3D highlight and shadow effects.

## Theme

The second tablets (luchot shniyot) were given on Yom Kippur — after the first tablets were shattered on the seventeenth of Tammuz. The piece connects to Shavuot through the covenant's capacity to renew after rupture: every Shavuot we re-enter a relationship that has already survived its worst moment.

## Technique

Grid-based Celtic knotwork generation using a 5×9 crossing grid per tablet. At each grid crossing, the over/under alternates by position parity, creating an interlaced strand pattern. The strand is rendered as a 3D ribbon (shadow + lapis base + gold highlight). A 5-second sinusoidal breathing animation pulses the strand width ±4%.

## Palette

- Background: warm parchment `#F2E8D0`
- Strand base: deep lapis lazuli `#1A3A6B`
- Highlight: gold `#C8A000`
- Shadow: near-black `#0A0A18`
